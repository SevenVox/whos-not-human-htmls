Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Who's Not Human is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Do Not Collect

Who's Not Human does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data or GPS information
  • No device identifiers or usage statistics
  • No browsing history or search queries
  • No photos, videos, or microphone access
  • No contact lists or social media accounts

Local Data Storage

All game progress, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to any external servers
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party services, including:

  • No advertising networks (AdMob, Unity Ads, etc.)
  • No analytics tools (Firebase Analytics, Flurry, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services (Crashlytics, etc.)
  • No social media SDKs
  • No payment processing services

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users aged 9 and above. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we collect no data whatsoever, there is no risk of children's information being gathered.

Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no data security risks associated with personal information. Your game data remains entirely on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. To remove all local game data, simply uninstall the application from your device.
