Frequently Asked Questions

Game Progress Lost?

Game data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, all progress will be lost. We recommend completing the game before switching devices.

App Crashes?

Please try restarting the app. If the issue persists, contact us with your device model and iOS version.

Language Support?

The game supports English and Simplified Chinese. It automatically detects your device language settings.
